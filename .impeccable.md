## Design Context

### Users
Creative technologists and developers with a sense of humor.

### Brand Personality
Absurdist Bureaucratic Playfulness. Deadpan, precise, meticulously designed.

### Aesthetic Direction
Vintage government forms meets modern brutalism.

### Design Principles
1. Institutional precision for absurd content
2. Deadpan presentation of ridiculous ideas
3. Bureaucratic warmth through paper tones and typewriter fonts
